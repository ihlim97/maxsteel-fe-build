Company – Milestone
 

Milestone

2010
  • Conversion of Quality Management System to ISO 9001:2008 standards.
2008
  • Acquisition of neighbouring factory unit for production expansion. MaxSteel Enterprise now occupies 10,100 m2 in land area in Singapore.
  • MaxSteel Enterprise awarded with the Singapore SME 500 Company award.
  • Commissioning of 1 unit of 1,600mm wide fully automatic shearing line and 1 unit of 120mm x 1,250mm x 2,500mm fully automatic aluminium plate sawing machine in MaxSteel (Suzhou).
2007
  • Installation of 3 units of 5 Tons and 1 unit of 10 Tons overhead cranes to further enhance material handling capacity in MaxSteel (Suzhou).
  • Expansion of mother coil warehousing facilities in MaxSteel (Suzhou).
2006
  • Commissioning of 1 unit of mini-shearing line in Singapore.
2005
  • MaxSteel Corporation (Suzhou) commences full operation.
  • MaxSteel Enterprise celebrates 10 years of incorporation.
2004
  • Incorporation of MaxSteel Corporation (Suzhou) in the People’s Republic of China.
  • Extension of factory to increase loading and unloading capacity in Singapore.
2003
  • Extension of factory to build new mezzanine office floor and roof top garden for staff recreation area in Singapore.
  • Commissioning of 1 unit of 1,350mm cut-to-length (4’ x 8’) levelling line in Singapore.
2002
  • MaxSteel Enterprise awarded with PSB ISO 9001:2000 certification.
  • Commissioning of 3 units of high speed precision sawing machines in Singapore.
2001
  • Commencement of operations in new acquired factory unit.
  • Commissioning of 1 unit of slitting line with belt-bridle facility.
2000
  • Acquisition of new factory unit at 74 Senoko Drive.
1999
  • Commissioning of 1 unit of slitting line with complete steel processing facilities.
1998
  • Incorporation of joint-venture company, European Trafo Products, with Cedaspe s.p.a. (Italy) and Webster Wilkinson Limited (United Kingdom) to begin stocking and distribution of transformer accessories for South-East Asian markets.
1997
  • Commissioning of 1 unit of shearing line to commence steel processing facility.
1995
  • Incorporation of MaxSteel Enterprise Private Limited by founders, Mr Henry Ng and Mdm Judy Tan.
  • MaxSteel Enterprise begins operations as a steel stockist and trading company.
