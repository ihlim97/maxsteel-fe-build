Services – Precision Circular Sawing
 

Precision Circular Sawing

The precise division of shaped bars length-wise into pieces of the required lengths or weights. Product pieces are usually used in blanking, drawing or cold forging for industries including hard disk drive industry.

The automatic circular sawing machines utilize air pressure to control the feeding of materials accurately thus products are produced with good length tolerance.

To ensure strict dimensional control, measurements have to be taken during production. Control factors like piece weight or length, are critical to most customers; precision weighing scales are sensitive to slightest surrounding air movements thus the need for an environment with minimal air circulation. Recognising the need, sawing takes place in a clean and air-conditioned environment within the factory.
