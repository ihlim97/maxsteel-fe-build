Affiliations

ETP’s affiliate companies are well established in the power distribution market; ETP is proud to be an affiliation as well as a distributor for their products.


Legnano Teknoelectric Company



LTC S.p.A. is the worldwide leader in processing of electrical steel and manufacturing of magnetic cores for transformers. The company was founded in 1983 by the Bertelli family and has acquired knowledge and experience through the design and manufacture of electrical transformers.

With continuous developments in reaching the highest quality standards, LTC now specializes in processing grain oriented electrical steel, high permeability electrical steel, laser treated electrical steel and non-oriented electrical steel on 4 different production plants, each dedicated to a different process.

The company provides full range of products including slit to width, cut laminations and built cores for the manufacture of power and distribution transformers, traction transformers, furnace transformers, dry type low voltage transformers and high voltage line reactors.

The production cycle is operated through the most advanced automation lines for both the processing of electrical steel and the production of steels clamps needed for the assembly of fully built cores.


Webster-Wilkinson



Webster-Wilkinson Limited began manufacturing high voltage porcelain insulated bushings in 1966. Products manufactured are in accordance with the British and Industry standards. The company supplied to well known companies within the United Kingdom and grew by acquisition over the years.

Well established in purpose built premises, the company remains at the forefront of high voltage electrical design and manufacture.


C.A.P.T



C.A.P.T. s.r.l. produces tap changers for current distribution transformers. The core business of C.A.P.T. is divided in two parts: Off-Circuit tap changers for distribution oil transformers and Off-Circuit tap changers for power oil transformers.

The company is divided into 3 modern plants with highly automated machineries and assembly lines. The use of automated technology increases production capacity, improves product quality and raises efficiency of the company.

In addition, thanks to experience of over 30 years with the products and the collaboration with suppliers, C.A.P.T. has installed a very innovative and state-of-the-art-automation system specifically customized for the making of tap changers.
