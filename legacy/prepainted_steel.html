Prepainted Steel

Background

Prepainted steel is basically steel that is manufactured with color at the mill. There is a wide variety in base metal which includes hot dipped galvanized steel, electrogalvanized steel, cold rolled steel, galvannealed steel, galvalume steel, stainless steel and many others.

Prepainted steel is usually coated or baked with paint containing polyester resin. Usually, primer, chromate and zinc coats are added between the paint finish and base metal.

Characteristics

The general appearance of prepainted steel is offered in a wide array of colors and polish. The common attributes will follow that of its base metal but generally displays superior workability, durability, and weather resistance.

Applications

Prepainted steel is mainly used for exterior panels and interior design purposes that do not require excessive processing.
