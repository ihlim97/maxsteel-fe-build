Message From MD

Welcome to the MaxSteel Group of Companies.

We are a dynamic and growing organization in the ferrous and non-ferrous processing industry offering a one-stop-supply solution to the electro-mechanical and hard disk drive industries.

Our devotion and unyielding spirit throughout our course of business has earned us great reputation as the most resourceful, quick response and action supplier among our competitors.

However, we always remember what Warren Buffet had also said about reputation. He said “It takes 20 years to build a reputation, whereas only 5 minutes to lose it.”

Our Vision

To be the top preferred metal processing and trading center that supports metal product engineering solution providers in the world.

Our Mission

To continually challenge ourselves in sourcing and supplying unique products and services with excellent quality and competitive pricing for the mutual benefit of our customers.

Our journey of challenges will not be an easy one but we will continue our efforts to remain competitive, actively listening to our customers and to ready ourselves for the future.

Finally, I would like to express my sincere gratitude to all our customers, suppliers and employees who have helped to make MaxSteel today.


Henry Ng

Managing Director
MaxSteel Group
