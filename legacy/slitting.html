Services – Slitting
 

Slitting

The division of a coil length-wise into narrower strips in the same direction in which the coil has been rolled or unrolled.

Our slitting is well equipped with the Belt-Bridle Tensioning System that not only prevents product coil elongation but also effectively reduces surface scratches even on very delicate materials which are used in the hard disk drive industries today.

Paper Interleaving or Lamination is offered to customers who have stringent cosmetic requirements.

Rotary slitting knives used are of high grade and sharpened regularly to ensure consistent product quality.

Quality inspections and records are mandatory for all job orders to ensure that the product coils are kept within tight dimensional tolerances and surface requirements. Our quality records also allow traceability of every finished product.
