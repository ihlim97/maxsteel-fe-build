Company – Company Profile: MaxSteel Singapore
 

MaxSteel Singapore

MaxSteel Enterprise Private Limited is a Singapore based steel processing center incorporated on 29th May 1995. Since inception in 1995, MaxSteel has been providing quality steel products and excellent services to various established companies in the electrical, electronic, automotive and fabrication industries. To better facilitate customers with China operations, a fully owned subsidiary, MaxSteel Corporation (Suzhou), was inaugurated on 17th February 2004 in the Peopleâ€™s Republic of China.

The main business activities of the company are supplying and distributing of ferrous and non-ferrous products in slitted or sheared form, providing steel processing services and as general importer, exporter and indenter of materials.

MaxSteel is well known for capable productivity and flexibility to meet the requirements of customers with Just-In-Time (JIT) inventory business strategies. The company established a reputation over the past years as a reliable and trustworthy material supplier, supported with numerous accolades and testimonials.

The company is ISO 9001 certified since 2002, indicating an accepted quality management system being applied. The expansion and development of facilities has led the Singapore factory size to stand more than 10,000 m2 in land area.

Today, MaxSteel Enterprise continues to provide the highest level of services and strives to make MaxSteel a quality name in the metal supply industry.
