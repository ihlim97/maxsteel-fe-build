Paper Interleaving

The process where special thin paper is recoiled together with the material between every layer to prevent inter-layer abrasions or scratches.

Some grades of materials like stainless steels or aluminiums have very critical surface finishes and can flaw easily. To protect surface finish on these materials while processing (slitting), special thin papers are coiled in between material layers as interleaves during recoiling. Paper interleaving is a good method to prevent surface defects.
